Welcome to our mileage calculator! This tool will help you calculate how many miles your students are racking up, whether they're walking/ running for time, covering a fixed distance, or using pedometers to track their steps. Post those miles to your school's profile and then get pumped to do even more!

CALCULATE

minutes to miles

Do you have kids running for a certain amount of time but don't track distance? All those miles can count in the Billion Mile Race. Convert those minutes to miles and post them to your school’s profile.

distance to miles

Lots of schools hold fun runs, walk-a-thons, fitness sessions, or other activities where kids all run the same distance. Convert that activity to miles you can log in Billion Mile Race.

steps to miles

If your kids wear pedometers during walking/ running activities, convert those steps into miles and add them to your school’s mileage total.
