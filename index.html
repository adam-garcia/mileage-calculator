Mileage Calculator

Welcome to our mileage calculator! This tool will help you calculate how many miles your students are racking up, whether they're walking/ running for time, covering a fixed distance, or using pedometers to track their steps. Post those miles to your school's profile and then get pumped to do even more!/p>

Walking or Running for Time

Do you have kids running for a certain amount of time but don't track distance? All those miles can count in the Billion Mile Race! Use this calculator to estimate how many miles kids at your school have covered.

Walking or Running over a Fixed Distance

Lots of schools hold fun runs, walk-a-thons, fitness sessions, or other activities where kids all run the same distance. Use this nifty tool to convert that activity to miles you can post to your school's Billion Mile Race profile.

Walking or Running with Pedometers

If your kids wear pedometers during walking/ running activities, you can use this tool to convert those steps into miles you can log to your school's profile.
