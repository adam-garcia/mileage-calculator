About this calculation
×

Different kids walk, run, and jog at different speeds. For this calculator, we used pretty conservative estimates of average pace for each of the different pace categories. Here’s what we used for each one:

  • Easy walk: 2.2 miles/hour
  • Moderate walk: 2.8 miles/hour
  • Fast walk: 3.4 miles/hour
  • Easy jog: 4.7 miles/hour
  • Moderate jog: 5.6 miles/hour
  • Fast jog: 6.6 miles/hour

The formula for estimating miles, then, is:

$$ Miles = \frac{kids \times duration}{pace} \times \# times $$

We made the estimates based on research describing typical walking and running speeds in children and teens. Want to check out the paper? Here it is! Ridley K, Ainsworth BE, Olds TS. Development of a compendium of energy expenditures for youth. International Journal of Behavioral Nutrition and Physical Activity. 2008 Sep 10;5(1):45. [View on PubMed]
