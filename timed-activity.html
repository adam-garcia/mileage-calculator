About this calculation
×

We'd like to be as transparent as possible with these calculators, so here's a quick breakdown! First off, take a quick look at the equation we use to estimate miles:

$$ Miles = \frac{kids \times time}{pace} \times groups $$ Not so bad, right? You entered the number of kids as well as the time, and "How many times..." is the number of groups. You might be wondering where the "pace" comes from, though. The table below shows the cutpoints we used, which come from this article from the International Journal of Behavioral Nutrition and Physical Activity.
Entered Pace Walking speed Mile pace from speed
Light walk 2.2 mph 27.5 min/mile
Moderate walk 2.81 mph 21.4 min/mile

Moderate jog 5.63 mph 10.7 min/mile
