About this Calculation
×

We'd like to be as transparent as possible with these calculators, so here's a quick breakdown! First off, take a quick look at the equation we use to estimate miles:

$$ Miles = \frac{kids \times steps}{2470} \times groups $$ Not so bad, right? You entered the number of kids as well as the average number of steps, and "How many times..." is the number of groups. You might be wondering where 2470 comes from, though. This article found that the average step length in 6-12 year old children was 0.6479 m at a fast paced walk. There are about 1600 meters in a mile, so: $$ \frac{1600m/mile}{0.6479 m/step} = 2470steps/mile $$
