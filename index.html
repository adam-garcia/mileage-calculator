BMR Mileage Calculator

To use the calculator, first select the type of activity you are logging from the options below:

Warmup Laps or Fitness

Estimate mileage from timed warmup activities.

Walk to School

If a number of your students walk to school, click this tool to estimate their mileage from commuting.

Fun run

Use this tool to estimate the total mileage students log during a community fun run/ 5k.

Walk/ Jogathon

If your students participated in a community fun-run, where time was tracked rather than miles, use this tool.

Pedometer-measured activity

If your students track steps with a pedometer, use this tool to estimate their mileage from steps

Fitness Testing

Use these tools to estimate your students' mileage from fitness evaluation tests.
